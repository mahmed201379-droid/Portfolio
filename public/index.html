About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate from Bangladesh with experience spanning both academic research and applied AI development. My academic work explored deep learning and medical image analysis, focusing on attention mechanisms, hybrid architectures, and diagnostic systems for medical imaging. I have published research on brain tumor classification, early system fault detection, and intelligent healthcare applications, as well as applied studies in predicting physical movement intensity from sensor data and analyzing ride-sharing request patterns to optimize allocation strategies.

Currently, I work as an AI Engineer at EATL Innovation Hub Ltd, specializing in Large Language Models (LLMs), ,advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and agentic AI systems. My projects include developing a AI-powered medical learning system and building intelligent agents that integrate reasoning, planning, and retrieval for practical deployment across real-world domains.
