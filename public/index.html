About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate from Bangladesh, passionate about building real-world AI solutions. My research journey started in deep learning and medical AI, but now my primary focus has shifted to Large Language Models (LLMs) and advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). I have developed applications like a RAG-powered academic assistant and am exploring agentic AI systems that combine reasoning, planning, and retrieval for real-world deployment.

My long-term goal is to pursue a research-based master's degree, secure a research internship at a Big Tech company, and eventually launch my own AI startup that combines LLMs, RAG, and real-world applications to make a global impact.

Research Focus

My research journey began in deep learning and medical image analysis, where I explored attention mechanisms, hybrid CNN models, and intelligent diagnostic systems. Over time, my focus has shifted towards Large Language Models (LLMs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), reflecting the global shift towards explainable and human-centered AI.

Currently, I am working on developing advanced RAG architectures, including multimodal RAG, agentic RAG, and agentic AI systems that combine reasoning, planning, and retrieval. These approaches aim to bridge the gap between autonomous AI agents and practical real-world applications, especially in education, healthcare, and research support.

My broader vision is to contribute to building the next generation of AI-powered knowledge systems that are reliable, interpretable, and capable of augmenting human decision-making in critical domains.
